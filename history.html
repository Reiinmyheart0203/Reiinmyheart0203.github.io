Five facts you need to know about Boulevard

  1. Hot Wheels Boulevard launched in 2012 and 2013, succeeding Hot Wheels Garage in 2011.
  2. Starting in 2020, the Boulevard returns with the Hot Wheels Premium label.
  3. The 2012 line was broken into six themed groups (Time, Big Hits, Concept Cars, Legends, Show Rods, and Underdogs).
  4. The 2020 line is still being updated so far, and nineteen series have been updated.
  5. The entire Boulevard series is produced in Malaysia and Thailand.
